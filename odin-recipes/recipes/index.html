Lasagna Recipe

Prepare

This recipe has been in my family many years. I am very excited to share it with you. I am confident that when you try this you will never want to use another recipe again.

So let's start. You will need a large casserol dish, sour cream, ground beef, lasagna noodles and italian seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef: 1.5 lbs
  • Sour Cream: 1 pint
  • Lasagna: 1 box
  • Mozzerella Cheese: 2 cups
  • Ragu Sauce: 1 jar
  • Italian Seasoning: As much as you like!

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Boil lasagna until soft enough to roll
  3. Mix sour cream, cheese, and ground beef in a mixing bowl
  4. Spread mix on lasagna and roll
  5. Place rolls in casserol dish
  6. Pour ragu sauce over rolls and sprinkle cheese on top
  7. Bake covered on 350 degrees for 45 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
